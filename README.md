# Daily Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard was developed to monitor daily sales performance, target achievement, dealer network productivity, and product penetration across zones, regions, and territories. The solution provides real-time visibility into sales KPIs and helps management track progress against business targets.

--- 
## Business Objectives

The project was designed to answer key business questions:

* How is actual sales performing against targets?
* What is the NCR, Plus %, and PP % achievement?
* Which regions and zones contribute the highest sales?
* How many active and effective dealers/retailers are engaged?
* Which regions are lagging behind target achievement?
* How is dealer network productivity performing? 

---

## Dashboard Sections

### 1. Sales Performance Overview

Provides a high-level summary of:

* Actual Quantity (MTD)
* Prorata Target
* Achievement %
* Total Target
* NCR
* Premium Product %

---

### 2. Dealer Network Status

Analyzes:

* Active Dealers
* Active Retailers
* Effective Dealers
* Effective Retailers
* PP Dealers & Retailers
* Plus Dealers & Retailers
* Zero Dealers Sales

---

### 3. Sales Trend Analysis

Monitors:

* Active vs Effective Dealers
* Region-wise Dealer Performance
* Daily Sales Movement

---
### 4. Regional Performance Analysis

Provides:

* Region-wise NCR
* Plus %
* PP %
* AW %
* Sales Achievement Comparison

---

### 5. Zone-wise Sales Distribution

Explores:

* Sales contribution by Zone
* Regional sales share
* Sales vs Prorata Target comparison

* Tracked daily sales achievement against prorata targets.
